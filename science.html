Science

The field of science encompasses the systematic study of the natural world, including its physical, chemical, and biological aspects. Through observation, experimentation, and analysis, scientists seek to understand the underlying principles governing phenomena in Science contributes to technological advancements, medical breakthroughs, and environmental conservation efforts.
